# Alex Hormozi — Value Equation & Offer Framework

## The Value Equation

```
Value = (Dream Outcome × Perceived Likelihood) / (Time Delay × Effort & Sacrifice)
```

Maximize the top. Minimize the bottom. Apply to every section of the page.

## Dream Outcome (maximize)

What does the customer ACTUALLY want? Not the feature — the transformation.

| Don't say | Say instead | Why it works |
|---|---|---|
| "Earn money from reviews" | "$247.80 this month" | Specific number = believable |
| "Get discovered" | "89 people saved your restaurant this week" | Concrete outcome they can picture |
| "Build your brand" | "2,847 followers trust your taste" | Social proof = tangible status |

**On the page:** Show the end state visually. Mock dashboards showing real
earnings beat feature descriptions every time.

## Perceived Likelihood (maximize)

Does the customer believe they'll actually get the result?

**Tactics that work on landing pages:**
- Testimonials with real names, real stats, real photos, real cities
- Comparison tables: old way vs. new way, side by side
- Proof of mechanism: explain WHY it works, not just THAT it works
- Risk reversal: "free forever", "no credit card", "cancel anytime"
- Show someone LIKE THEM succeeding: match demographic, match situation

## Time Delay (minimize)

How long until they see results?

**Tactics:**
- "Earn from day one" / "First payout in 30 days" / "Paid monthly"
- Show immediate value at signup, not just a promise of future value
- Specific timelines: "30 days" not "soon" or "eventually"

## Effort & Sacrifice (minimize)

How much work or behavior change is required?

**Tactics:**
- Frame as zero behavior change: "Same habit. Now it pays."
- Compare effort to what they already do: "You already write reviews for free"
- Remove signup friction: email only, no credit card, instant access
- "3 simple steps" — make the path feel effortless

## Grand Slam Offer Template

For CTAs, combine all four elements:

"Get [DREAM OUTCOME] in [TIME FRAME] without [EFFORT] or [RISK REVERSAL]"

**Examples:**
- "Earn money from your reviews in 30 days without changing anything — free forever"
- "Get 10 qualified leads per week without cold calling — cancel anytime"
- "Launch a site in 60 seconds without code — no credit card needed"

## Urgency & Scarcity

Real urgency converts. Fake urgency destroys trust forever.

**Legitimate:**
- Limited founding spots tied to real benefit (guaranteed minimum payout)
- Early pricing that genuinely increases later (announce the future price)
- Capacity constraints (only launching in 3 cities first)
- Time-limited bonuses tied to real operational reasons

**Never do:**
- Countdown timers that reset on refresh
- "Only 3 left!" with unlimited supply
- Manufactured scarcity with no actual constraint behind it
